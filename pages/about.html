A little about me.

I'm Todd Greco — a product and brand designer based in Seattle, WA. I've spent over a decade helping companies find clarity in their visual voice and build design systems that last.

Story

Designing with purpose, always.

Design found me early. Growing up, I was drawn to how things looked — the curve of a letterform, the weight of a line, the way two colors either fought or sang together. What started as a personal obsession became a decade-long creative practice.

I studied graphic design at the University of Washington, then spent several years at top agencies in Seattle and San Francisco, working across brand, digital, and environmental design. That breadth taught me how to move fluidly between strategy and execution — thinking big while sweating the details.

"Good design is honest. It doesn't shout — it simply makes you feel understood."

Today I work independently with a select roster of clients — from early-stage startups to established brands ready for a refresh. My process is always collaborative: I treat every brief as a conversation, not a directive.

Outside of work, you'll find me hiking the Cascades, haunting used bookshops, and arguing passionately about typeface choices with anyone who will listen. Currently available for new projects starting Q2 2026.
